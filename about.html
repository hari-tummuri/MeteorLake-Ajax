Legal Research Assistant  Powered by Gen-AI

Welcome to our innovative application designed to revolutionize your legal research experience. As a dedicated AI legal research assistant, we aim to streamline and simplify your professional journey with our robust and intuitive features.

Our platform is designed with a user-friendly interface where lawyers have access to a personalized My Hub. My Hub provides a calendar which indicates the schedule of the lawyer and also contains the details of the cases related to what he handles and helping the lawyer to drastically reduce research time.

Legal Cases Page consist of detailed table related to cases which the lawyer handles and allows to filter and sort the case details in different permutations and combinations.. When the lawyer clicks on the document number , it takes to another screen which enables chat with the document, which functions based on cutting edge retrieval augmented generation technology powered by powerful LLM models. Cherry on the cake, the lawyer can view the summary of the document just by a single click.

Profile page consist of key personal details of lawyer and a chart which shows the career track record like cases won , lost and the cases in progress helping the particular lawyer to keep track of the career path.

One of our unique features is the Legal Answer Engine page. Here, you can access a list of documents relevant to a particular case with just a simple click on the case number from the My Hub Page or Legal Cases Page. The Legal Answer Engine is your personal interactive space where you can select any document from the list to gain insights. This page is generally used by lawyers to get the crux of the long document. Instead of wasting time reading lengthy documents, with this LLM powered tool, just by few clicks to upload the document, the lawyer can attain key takeaways from the document, increasing his productivity.

Chat with Assistant

(Chat with Assistant to get insights about the case documents)

Utilizing state-of-the-art technology, our Chat feature allows you to interact directly with your case documents. Our Retriever-and-Generator (RAG) system employs Jina AI's jinaai-text-embeddings-v2-base for text embedding. Text is converted into vector representations for effective data processing. These vectors are stored in a specialized database for efficient and quick retrieval. The Llama 2 7B model, a key component of the RAG system, processes these vectors. The model is optimized using Intel's OpenVINO toolkit, enhancing the speed and efficiency of inferencing. This optimization significantly reduces the inference time to just 7 seconds.

Get Summary

(Get summary about the case documents)

Our unique "Get Summary" feature uses the Falconsai/text_summarization model to condense lengthy legal case documents into clear and concise summaries. The model was specifically chosen due to its excellent performance in summarizing extensive documents. It is ideally suited for applications that involve condensing comprehensive legal texts, enabling users to quickly understand the key points of the documents without having to read through the entire text. The use of this model significantly enhances efficiency in the legal sector.
